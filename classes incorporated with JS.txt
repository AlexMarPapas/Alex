Classes that they are incorporated with JS after specific actions from the users.

.section-page-active (class that being applied in the Cards (resume , skills , portofolio etc)
when the user click on any of them so we know which card is active and displaying and take a
specific style.)

.main-menu-pgactive (class that being applied in the main-menu when the user is clicking 
anywhere in the menu BUT Home)

.section-vcardbody-pgactive (class that being applied only in the first Card , so when the user
is viewing any of the other cards the first card stays active and displayed on the left)

.menuActive (class that being applied in every menu section that is active.For example
if the user is diplaying any section(portfolio,skills etc) then the button for this section stays
ACTIVE.)
